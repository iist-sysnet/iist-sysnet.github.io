Research Areas

Systems and Networks Lab (SNL) is part of Department of Avionics, Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology, focuses on the research activities in the broader area of computer and communication networks. Major research areas include:

  • Complex Networks
  • Delay Tolerant Networks (DTN)
  • Software Defined Networks (SDN)
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Wireless Mesh Networks (WMN)
  • Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN)
  • Graph Signal Processing
  • Network Security
  • Quantum Computing
